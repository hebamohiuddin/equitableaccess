Mission

Equitable Access is a legislative campaign that is on a mission to fight misinformation and discrimination to finally bring an end to the HIV/AIDS epidemic. With 800,000 American lost and rising rates of HIV, we must act now and ensure equal and free access to HIV-preventing medication (PrEP) for all Americans.
